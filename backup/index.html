Flower Care Tips

Watering

Water most flowers 1-2 times weekly, ensuring soil is moist but not waterlogged.

Sunlight

Most flowers need 6-8 hours of sunlight daily; check specific needs.

Soil

Use well-draining soil; add compost for nutrients.
